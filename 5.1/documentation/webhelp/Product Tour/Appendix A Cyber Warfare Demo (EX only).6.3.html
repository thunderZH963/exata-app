A.2.1 Launching the Demo Scenario in EXata
Start the CyberWarfareDemo scenario in EXata as follows:
1.
Load the file CyberWarfareDemo.config in EXata and click the Initialize Emulation button in the toolbar. Use the Zoom and Pan buttons to magnify and center the scenario.
